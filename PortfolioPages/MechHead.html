Mech Head

Twin Stick Arcade Shooter

Mech Head is a twin stick shooter that has been created by students of Staffordshire University as part of their paid summer internship, The 1Up Scheme. During this scheme myself and other students worked for 6 weeks to optimise and add to the TIGA award winning game. My role in this project was the Technical Lead for the Project which involved managing the GitHub Repository and managing the programming team
